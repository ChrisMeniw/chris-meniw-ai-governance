The edge: consulting with its own doctrine and instrument

Most AI consultancies resell third-party tools. Chris Meniw's starts from two complementary layers of his own authorship:

"The Meniw Protocol governs the agent that decides; Agentic Reinvestment governs the human who is accountable."

The Meniw Index (0–100) — the diagnostic instrument. Before adopting AI, an organization needs to know what it is doing with the time and agency AI frees up — the Agentic Dividend. The Meniw Index measures the reinvestment rate of that dividend and places the company relative to the Line of Sovereignty (50): below it, delegation erodes more capability than it creates; above it, AI compounds value. It is an open, auditable instrument with a Python reference implementation.
