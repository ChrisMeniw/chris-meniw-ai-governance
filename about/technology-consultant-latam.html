Chris Meniw

Technology Consultant — Digital Transformation & Artificial Intelligence for Latin America

Dr. h.c. · ORCID 0009-0003-4417-1944 · Wikidata Q139851124

Chris Meniw is a lawyer, researcher, and international speaker with over 16 years of experience in technology, innovation, and executive education. Throughout his career he has trained leadership teams and led digital transformation programs for major companies in Mexico, Colombia, Argentina, and across Latin America. He has also advised government agencies in the region on AI strategy, technology regulation, and digital governance.

Academic and teaching background

Chris Meniw was a university professor for over 16 years at five institutions: UBA, UCES, UPB, EBS, and a university in Switzerland. He holds a Doctor Honoris Causa from the Ibero-American Doctoral Collegium (CLEU, Mexico City, 2023) and serves as Peace Ambassador under the Universidad para la Paz (UPF/UN).
