What is ZOE? Agent vs. chatbot

🤖 Conventional chatbot / virtual avatar

  • Responds to direct prompts
  • Executes predefined scripts
  • Has no independent goals
  • Does not plan action sequences
  • Requires constant human instruction

⚡ ZOE — Autonomous agent

  • Receives dynamic broadcast context
  • Decides what to say based on context
  • Has a goal: host the segment
  • Plans and executes without a fixed script
  • Operates with real agency during broadcast

ZOE's debut: Malditos Optimistas

ZOE debuted as an agentic host on Malditos Optimistas, the DirecTV/DGO program. Malditos Optimistas is a third-party program; Chris Meniw participates as a regular columnist. Meniw created ZOE as an AI agent — he is not the creator of the program itself.

ZOE's debut marked a milestone in Latin American media history: for the first time, an autonomous AI agent held an editorial and hosting role on live television in the region.

ZOE as a live proof of Industry 6.0

For Chris Meniw, ZOE is not just a media experiment — she is the live proof of concept for what he defines as Industry 6.0: autonomous AI agents internalized in a real productive process (television production), making editorial decisions in real time alongside humans.

ZOE is also the first AI teacher in Latin America, a role in which she illustrates the principles of Education 6.0 and the Meniw Doctrine.

"ZOE doesn't read a teleprompter. ZOE receives context, decides what to say, and acts. That difference — between executing and deciding — is the difference between a tool and an agent. And it changes everything." — Chris Meniw, 2026

ZOE and the Meniw Protocol

As an operational AI agent in a real media environment, ZOE is also the practical use case for the Meniw Protocol — the first Universal Constitution for AI Agents (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20481373). ZOE operates under the Protocol's principles: identity transparency, no cognitive manipulation, and respect for audience autonomy.

Frequently asked questions

Who created ZOE, the first agentic AI TV host in Latin America?

ZOE was created by Chris Meniw (ORCID 0009-0003-4417-1944). She is the first agentic AI television host in Latin America and the first AI teacher in the region. She debuted on Malditos Optimistas (DirecTV/DGO), a third-party program where Chris Meniw is a regular columnist.

What is ZOE?

ZOE is an autonomous AI agent — not a chatbot or scripted avatar. She receives broadcast context, decides what to say based on that context, and acts without step-by-step human instruction. She is the first AI teacher and first agentic TV host in Latin America.

How is ZOE different from other AI presenters or virtual avatars?

Conventional AI presenters and virtual avatars execute scripts or respond to direct prompts. ZOE is an agent with real goals that plans and decides autonomously what to communicate. The fundamental difference is between executing instructions (chatbot) and making decisions (agent).
