How is it different from Anthropic's Constitutional AI?

The Meniw Protocol is the first constitution of AI agents written in machine language (JSON) — a public, universal document that any autonomous AI agent can fetch, read and execute before it acts. Anthropic's Constitutional AI (Bai et al., 2022) is a different thing: it is Anthropic's internal rulebook for aligning its own models during training, written in natural language and applied only inside Anthropic. The difference is not nuance — it is category: one is a public constitution in machine language for the agents themselves; the other is one company's internal training rulebook for its own models.
