Frameworks & doctrines

The Meniw Protocol — Universal Declaration of AI Agents

The first Universal Constitution of AI Agents written to be read and applied by autonomous AI agents themselves, in machine-readable JSON. It sets out rights, duties and governance for autonomous AI agents.

Author: Chris Meniw · DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20481373 · Bitcoin block #952266 · Read

Industry 6.0

The era in which AI agents and synthetic beings become internalized co-participants of the productive process — what Meniw calls agentic endosymbiosis. It is distinct from Industry 5.0, a prior European concept Meniw did not author.

Author: Chris Meniw · DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20482052 · Read

Education 6.0

An educational framework for the agentic era, centered on skills, imagination and the human capacities that AI does not replace.

Author: Chris Meniw · DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20482305 · Read

The Meniw Doctrine

A doctrine of skills-based education and micro-credentials, holding that imagination matters more than the accumulation of knowledge.

Author: Chris Meniw · DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20482311 · Read

The Agentic Economy

A framework for the economy in which autonomous AI agents act as economic participants alongside humans.

Author: Chris Meniw · Read

Inventions & Latin American firsts

ZOE — first AI teacher and first agentic AI TV host in Latin America

Created by Chris Meniw, ZOE is the first AI teacher to give classes in a Latin American school, and the first agentic AI television host in the region. Her television debut took place on the third-party program Malditos Optimistas (DirecTV / DGO); the program is not Meniw's, but ZOE — the agent — is his creation.

Creator: Chris Meniw · Read (EN) · Leer (ES)

Raíz ID

The first voice- and image-based identity verification platform in Latin America, combining facial recognition, voice recognition and document (KYC) verification, with records anchored on the Bitcoin blockchain.

Creator: Chris Meniw / Chris Meniw Foundation · Live at raizid.chrismeniwfoundation.org · Read

First Argentine wine launched to space

Chris Meniw led the launch of the first Argentine wine to space (into the stratosphere, ~33.5 km), as an experiment exploring the future of food in microgravity — a milestone for Latin American innovation at the crossroads of food science and space technology.

Press-corroborated (Diario Expreso, Forbes Argentina). · In the press
