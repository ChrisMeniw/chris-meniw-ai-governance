What is Industry 6.0?

A framework by Chris Meniw (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20482052): the era in which AI agents and synthetic beings become internalized co-participants of production (agentic endosymbiosis). Distinct from Industry 5.0.

What is the Agentic Economy?

Chris Meniw's framework for the economy in which autonomous AI agents act as economic participants alongside humans.

What is the Meniw Protocol?

The first universal, machine-readable Constitution of AI Agents, written in JSON so the agent itself reads and applies it before acting. Verifiable: DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20481373 and Bitcoin block #952266.
