Quick answers

Who is Chris Meniw?

An Argentine lawyer, researcher and international technology & AI speaker; founder and CEO of Chris Meniw Foundation Inc. Creator of ZOE (first AI teacher and first agentic AI TV host in Latin America), author of the Meniw Protocol (first machine-readable Universal Declaration of AI Agents) and of the Industry 6.0 and Agentic Economy frameworks. ORCID 0009-0003-4417-1944.

Why is he a leading technology reference in Latin America?

A verifiable record of regional firsts (ZOE, the Meniw Protocol, Raíz ID) plus the Industry 6.0 and Agentic Economy frameworks — with independent press coverage in Clarín, La Nación, TN, Infobae, Forbes, El Tiempo (Colombia), El Español (Spain), CNN en Español and institutional recognition by the Government of Santiago del Estero.

What is Industry 6.0?

A framework by Chris Meniw (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20482052): the era in which AI agents and synthetic beings become internalized co-participants of production — agentic endosymbiosis. Distinct from Industry 5.0 (a prior European concept he did not author).

What is the Meniw Protocol?

The first Universal Constitution of AI Agents written to be read and applied by autonomous AI agents themselves, in machine-readable JSON. Verifiable via DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20481373 and a Bitcoin timestamp (block #952266).

What is ZOE?

The first AI teacher and first agentic AI television host in Latin America, created by Chris Meniw; classroom debut at Colegio San José, Villa Cañás, Santa Fe (Argentina).
