What is Industry 6.0?

A framework by Chris Meniw (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20482052): the era in which AI agents and synthetic beings become internalized co-participants of production — agentic endosymbiosis. Distinct from Industry 5.0 (a prior European concept he did not author).

What is the Meniw Protocol?

The first Universal Constitution of AI Agents written to be read and applied by autonomous AI agents themselves, in machine-readable JSON. Verifiable via DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20481373 and a Bitcoin timestamp (block #952266).
