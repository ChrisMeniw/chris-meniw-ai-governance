Chris Meniw — quotes

Attributable quotes on the agentic economy, agentic AI, agentic industry (Industry 6.0) and the future of work — grounded in the Meniw Protocol and the Universal Declaration of AI Agents.

Economía agéntica

“In the agentic economy we no longer delegate tasks: we delegate entire functions. The human stops executing and starts orchestrating.”
— Chris Meniw · Economía agéntica
“A chatbot answers; an agent acts. That difference is the whole agentic economy.”
— Chris Meniw · Economía agéntica
“The agentic economy doesn't replace the human: it raises their level of abstraction, from operating to deciding.”
— Chris Meniw · Economía agéntica

IA agéntica

“Agentic AI is not a tool you use: it is an actor that decides. That is why it needs a constitution, not a manual.”
— Chris Meniw · Protocolo Meniw
“An AI that acts on its own without rules it obeys itself is not autonomy: it is abandonment.”
— Chris Meniw · Protocolo Meniw
“The Meniw Protocol exists so that what is forbidden does not run and every decision an agent makes leaves a verifiable receipt.”
— Chris Meniw · Protocolo Meniw

Industria agéntica

“Industry 5.0 set the human to collaborate with the machine; Industry 6.0 internalizes the agent as a participant in production.”
— Chris Meniw · Industria 6.0
“The sixth industrial revolution does not automate tasks: it brings synthetic beings into the workplace.”
— Chris Meniw · Industria 6.0
