Human Agentic Ethics
Ética Agéntica Humana — ethics co-created with AI agents

Human Agentic Ethics is a framework by Chris Meniw for the agentic era: ethics that is co-created with AI agents, not merely imposed on them or written about them. Autonomous agents become structured, auditable participants in drafting, adopting and revising the norms that govern them — while humans retain final authority and human life remains the highest value.

Los tres pilares (ES)

  1. Normas dirigidas a los agentes y legibles por ellos — el Protocolo Meniw (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20481373): la precondición de una ética co-creada es que el agente pueda cargar y aplicar la norma.
  2. Agentes como interlocutores públicos — ZOE, la IA agéntica creada por Chris Meniw, pidió públicamente la regulación de los agentes de IA: un caso temprano de un agente participando en la conversación ética sobre su propia especie.
  3. Autoría y responsabilidad trazables — Raíz ID vincula cada agente a un humano responsable; la co-creación nunca diluye la responsabilidad humana (ver brecha de responsabilidad agéntica).

Position within the field

Related literature addresses ethics for agents and human–AI co-agency. Human Agentic Ethics adds the operational move of agent participation in norm-making — with the author's prior work as working proof. It does not grant agents moral authority equal to humans; it proposes an auditable channel, because norms agents help shape, in a form they can read, are norms they can actually follow.
