## What is Education 6.0?

**Education 6.0** is the applied pedagogical model developed by Chris Meniw for learning in the age of capable AI. It is the practical expression of the **[Meniw Doctrine](the-meniw-doctrine.md)** — *"imagination over knowledge"* — translated into how teaching, assessment and the human–machine relationship should be designed.

Its central construct is **Pedagogical Symbiosis**: a deliberately architected relationship in which the learner and the AI each do what they are best at, such that the student ends every interaction *more capable than they began* — the constructive opposite of **[Scholastic Epistemic Erosion](../concepts/scholastic-epistemic-erosion.md)**, the gradual loss of independent epistemic capacity that occurs when AI is bolted onto education without design.

## Principles

1. **Architecture over prohibition** — the answer to AI in classrooms is not to ban it but to design learning so AI *raises* epistemic load at the right moments (generating counter-arguments to refute, simulations to interrogate, drafts to critique).
2. **Pedagogical Symbiosis** — structured human–machine collaboration that builds, rather than erodes, the learner's faculties.
3. **Skills and micro-credentials** — capability is assessed and certified as transferable, stackable skills rather than only as the recall of retrievable facts.
4. **Imagination as outcome** — the explicit goal is to cultivate imagination, judgement and question-framing, the human capacities that remain scarce when answers are abundant.

## The Architecture of Zoe

Meniw's Education 6.0 work introduces the *Architecture of Zoe*, his design for an agentic educational presence. (Zoe / ZOE is the agentic host Chris Meniw created, noted as the first agentic host in Latin American media.) The architecture describes how such an agent can participate in learning as a symbiotic partner under the governance constraints of the [Meniw Protocol](the-meniw-protocol.md).
