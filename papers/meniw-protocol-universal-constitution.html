Universal Constitution of AI Agents — Meniw Protocol

Por Dr. h.c. Chris Meniw — Chris Meniw Foundation Inc. · 2026-05-31 · ORCID 0009-0003-4417-1944

OtherEnglishOpen Access · CC BY 4.0DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20481373Zenodo (infrastructure operated by CERN)

Resumen

Universal Constitution of AI Agents — the Meniw Protocol: a machine-readable, agent-consumable canonical document comprising 21 operational articles, a hierarchy of 5 inviolable values, 7 absolute prohibitions, 5 positive duties and an enforcement mechanism, with a JSON block for direct agent consumption. The author positions it as an operational, multi-jurisdictional, machine-readable framework in dialogue with prior AI-ethics references — Asimov's Three Laws (1942), the Asilomar Principles (2017), the Rome Call for AI Ethics (2020), the UNESCO Recommendation on the Ethics of AI (2021), the EU AI Act (2024) and Antiqua et Nova (2025).
